About Me

Hello, I’m Percy and I am a Concept Artist, Illustrator and Visual designer from Vancouver, Canada. Currently I am in final year as a student at SFU’s School of Interactive Arts & Technology (SIAT) focusing on a Media Arts concentration.
I am passionate about both art and storytelling and wish blend the two to create expressive and memorable works. I believe that the art of simplicity holds a lot of power in the design world, both colour theory and basic geometric shapes have strong meaning in what they can convey about a work. I strive to create pieces that suit the appropriate experience they are made for.
I am skilled at drawing in Clip Studio Paint and Aseprite as well as designing with and the Adobe Software Suite. I also dabble with 3D work in Autodesk Maya and Unity.
I am looking for word in both illustration and User Interface and believe that my past experience has made me adaptable to learning new programs and working in team-based environments. Being a design leader in the past with the Skoden Indigenous Film Festival I’ve heavily used my organization and mentorship skills within my group. Likewise, doing illustration and programming work in a class visual novel assignment highlights my deep passion and investment into my work within the group.
