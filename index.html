About Me

Hello, I'm Percy I am passionate about creating seamless and captivating user experiences, utilizing design strategies to unravel complexity and introduce order. I have a keen eye for employing color theory and shape design, seamlessly weaving together visual elements to suit the appropriate experiences they are made for.
I am a graduate from Simon Fraser University with a Bachelor of Arts in Interactive Arts & Technology, specializing in Media Arts. My strengths lie in both graphic design and illustration.
From my experience I know I bring a proven adaptability to learning new programs and thrive in both collaborative, team-based environments as well as individual solo projects.
Outside of my professional pursuits, I maintain a keen interest in learning new languages, enjoy immersing myself in video games, and express my creativity by drawing my favorite characters.
