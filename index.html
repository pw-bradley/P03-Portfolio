About Me

Hello! I'm Percy, a Canadian UI and graphic designer with experience in illustration. I am skilled in creating visually appealing designs for digital and print formats. Accomplished in both design and coding, with notable achievements in crafting aesthetically pleasing and functional websites. I’m looking to grow as a designer and perfect my art prioritizing client satisfaction.
I graduated from Simon Fraser University’s School of Interactive Arts and Technology in Fall 2023 with a Bachelor of Arts, Concentration in Media Arts.
Beyond my professional pursuits, you'll catch me leading a book club with my friends, drawing, or immersing myself in diverse range of video games, ranging from harrowing dungeon crawlers to cozy farming simulators.
I can be contacted at percival.w.bradley@gmail.com.
