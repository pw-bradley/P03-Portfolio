About Me

Hi, I’m Percy and I’m a recent Simon Fraser University graduate with a Bachelor of Arts in Interactive Arts & Technology with a concentration in Media Arts. My strengths lie in UI design and illustration.
I am passionate about creating seamless and delightful user experiences using design strategies to help bring order to complexity. Likewise I love using colour theory and shape design to bring pieces together to suit the appropriate experiences they are made for.
I believe that my past experience has made me adaptable to learning new programs and working in team-based environments.
In my free time I like to keep up learn new languages, play video games and draw my favourite characters.
